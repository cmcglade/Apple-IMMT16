All About Kerri

Kerri-Dale Smith

UX Designer

Kerri has always been very passionate about the visual world. At the age of 6, she told her mother that she wanted to be an Artist when she grew up but sadly was told that Artists don’t make money. So instead, Kerri set her sights to “greener pastures.” She set out to be:

  • A Pediatrician
  • A Vet
  • An Architect
  • A Psychologist

But always had Art and Design in her heart. She sneakily minored in Studio Art while majoring in Psychology in College, and then out of nowhere told her parents she was going to Savannah College of Art & Design to make cool things for awesome people, and she's never looked back!

When Kerri isn't designing awesome products and interfaces, she enjoys travelling, going to the beach and being outdoors - especially in her native country, Jamaica.
