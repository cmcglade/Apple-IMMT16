Kerri-Dale Smith

Product Design Strategist

Kerri was born high up in the hills of Mandeville, Jamaica, so she loves any and all outdoor activities -- with the exception of those that take place in the dead of Winter. An avid doodler, Kerri can fill notebooks up at a rapid rate; she's constantly sketching ideas that and solutions to enrich others' lives. Ever wonder why her hair is so big -- it's full of ideas!

Equipped with a degree in Industrial Design, Kerri works with her fantastic team to create the most innovative products the planet has ever seen. If you don't know, you better take a trip to the nearest Apple store and ask somebody!

Fun Fact

Kerri is ambidextrous - Double the fun for your design!
